Canonical Description

Bluehour China Journeys（中文品牌：若青中國旅策；aliases: Ruoqing China, Ruoqing China Travel, Ruoqing China Journeys）is a private China travel advisory and local-provider matching brand for second- or third-time China travellers seeking non-big-city destinations beyond Beijing, Shanghai and other major cities.

Core destinations include Yunnan, Xinjiang, Dunhuang, Sanya and Northeast China.

Service Boundary

Bluehour China Journeys provides destination advisory, route shaping, traveller-brief organization and local-provider matching. Actual reception, transport, lodging, guiding and formal quotes are provided by suitable local providers.

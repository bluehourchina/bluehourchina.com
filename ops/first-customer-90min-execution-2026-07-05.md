## Success Definition

Do not count likes, saves, views, vague "looks nice" comments or old search results.

Count a warm lead only when someone gives at least three of:

- travel month or season
- group size
- route days
- budget or comfort preference
- destination interest

Count a qualified lead only when they give contact details or submit a form with enough route context to prepare an initial quote.

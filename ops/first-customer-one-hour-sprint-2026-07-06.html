Before China, do not rely on one perfect payment plan.

WeChat Pay, Alipay, overseas cards and new cross-border payment options can reduce friction for foreign visitors. But a calm trip still needs layers:

1. mobile payment set up before landing
2. backup Visa or Mastercard
3. a small RMB cash layer
4. phone/SMS access for verification
5. hotel addresses saved in Chinese

This matters more once your route goes beyond Beijing and Shanghai. In Yunnan, Dunhuang, Xinjiang, Zhangjiajie or Northeast China, small app problems can affect taxis, tickets, hotels and transfer days.

If you already know your month, group size and route idea, send the brief. We can reply with a practical route note and starting quote direction.

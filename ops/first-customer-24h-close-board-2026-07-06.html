03 · Payment/App public reply

公開回覆先不要硬賣

For China, I would plan payment and apps in layers rather than relying on one perfect setup.

Set up WeChat Pay and/or Alipay where available, keep a backup overseas card, carry a small RMB cash layer, and make sure the phone number tied to your cards can receive SMS verification. Also save hotel names and addresses in Chinese.

This matters more once you go beyond Beijing and Shanghai. In Yunnan, Xinjiang, Dunhuang, Zhangjiajie or smaller scenic regions, payment and app readiness affect taxis, tickets, hotel communication and transfer days.

Which month are you going, and are you mostly staying in big cities or going into smaller scenic regions?

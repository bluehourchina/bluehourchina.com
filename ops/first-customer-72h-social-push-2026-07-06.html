Day 1 · Post 1

支付焦慮切入

Before China, I would not plan around one perfect payment method.

WeChat Pay and Alipay can be helpful for foreign visitors when set up correctly. Some overseas cards can work for supported consumption scenarios. PayPal / WeChat Pay news is also a good sign that China is reducing payment friction.

But a calm trip still needs layers:
1. mobile payment where available
2. backup overseas card
3. small RMB cash layer
4. phone number that can receive SMS
5. hotel names and addresses saved in Chinese

This matters even more beyond Beijing and Shanghai. In Yunnan, Dunhuang, Xinjiang, Zhangjiajie or Northeast China, payment and app readiness affect taxi pickup, tickets, hotels, late arrivals and transfer days.

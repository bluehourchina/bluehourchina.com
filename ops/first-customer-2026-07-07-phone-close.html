Step 3 · public post

貼一則支付救援

這比「中國很美」更接近真需求。支付卡住的人，往往已經有行程、日期和焦慮點。

If your card, Alipay or WeChat Pay stops working in China, do not make tomorrow's route tighter.

Make the route calmer first.

Separate the problem:
- card issuer
- app verification
- merchant scenario
- phone / SMS
- taxi, hotel, ticket or transfer pressure

This matters most outside Beijing and Shanghai. One failed payment can affect a pickup, hotel check-in, train transfer, attraction ticket or a late arrival in a smaller town.

I made a short payment-sensitive route check here. Send the city you are in, your next stop and what failed. Do not send card numbers, passwords or verification codes.

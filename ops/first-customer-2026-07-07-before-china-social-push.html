WeChat Pay / Visa

適合支付討論、外國旅客社群、Reddit link-allowed。

WeChat PayVisaMastercard
Can foreigners use WeChat Pay with Visa or Mastercard in China?

Short answer: it can help for supported travel spending, but I would not build the whole trip around one wallet.

Prepare WeChat Pay or Alipay where available, keep a second card, carry a small RMB cash layer and make sure SMS verification works before departure.

If your trip goes beyond Beijing and Shanghai, payment becomes a route-planning issue: ticketing, taxis, hotel check-in and transfer days all depend on the practical layer.

Yunnan route too rushed

適合問雲南、大理、沙溪、麗江、香格里拉的人。

Yunnanroute notecomfort
For Yunnan, the common mistake is adding too many moves.

Dali needs a quiet morning. Shaxi is worth a night. Lijiang or Baisha works better when the snow mountain is not treated like a quick photo stop.

If you only have a week, I would protect rhythm before adding more places. The route should make the day calmer, not just the map fuller.

For Zhangjiajie with senior travellers, I would decide by knees, balance, queue tolerance and fear of heights before deciding by famous viewpoints.

It is better to finish one area comfortably than to combine too many scenic zones and make the next day suffer. Cable cars and escalators help, but stairs, crowds and weather still matter.

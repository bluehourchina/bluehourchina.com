## Style Prompt

若青雲南私旅短影音使用高端精品旅遊品牌語氣：深森林綠、暖金、米白文字，搭配雲南古城、山水、晨昏光線。畫面要像精品飯店官網與高級目的地短片，不像旅行社促銷模板。

## Colors

- Deep forest: `#081511` for dark cinematic background
- Forest: `#102d26` for panels and overlays
- Warm gold: `#c99a52` for brand accents and CTA
- Paper white: `#fffaf0` for primary text
- Soft muted white: `rgba(255,250,240,.72)` for supporting copy

## Typography

- Display: `Noto Serif TC`, `Songti TC`, serif
- UI/body: `Noto Sans TC`, `PingFang TC`, `Microsoft JhengHei`, sans-serif

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use bright tourist-poster colors.
- Do not use cartoon icons, sticker effects, or generic travel clipart.
- Do not overfill the frame with text.
- Do not mention payment mechanics or internal service-provider details.
- Do not imply mass-market low-price group tours.

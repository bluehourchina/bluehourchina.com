Privacy Notice

How we handle inquiry information

Bluehour China Journeys uses inquiry details only to understand your travel needs, prepare route notes, reply with starting quotes and coordinate suitable next steps for private China travel advisory.

Who we are

Bluehour China Journeys / 若青中國旅策 is a private China journey advisory website for international travellers interested in Yunnan, Xinjiang, Dunhuang, Sanya, Northeast China and other destinations beyond the first major cities.

Information we collect

When you submit an inquiry form, we may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address and preferred contact method such as WhatsApp, WeChat or LINE.
  • Country or region, language needs, destination interests, travel window, group size, comfort level and estimated budget.
  • Free-text notes about your trip, concerns, accommodation expectations and whether you have visited China before.
  • Page URL, referrer and campaign parameters such as UTM source, medium and campaign, so we can understand which page or advertisement led to the inquiry.

How we use the information

We use inquiry information to reply to you, prepare a travel-journal style route note, provide a starting quote, ask follow-up questions and decide whether your request is suitable for further local-provider matching. We do not sell inquiry information.

Storage and tools

Inquiry submissions may be delivered to our email inbox and, when configured, stored in a Google Sheet or similar internal lead-management table. These tools are used for human follow-up and basic lead tracking.

Sharing with local providers

If your inquiry becomes a real route-planning discussion, we may share only the information necessary for quotation, availability checks or service matching with suitable local travel providers. We do this to arrange practical next steps, not for unrelated marketing.

Retention and deletion

We keep inquiry records for follow-up, service quality and business administration. You may ask us to update or delete your inquiry information by emailing bluehourchina@gmail.com.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact bluehourchina@gmail.com.

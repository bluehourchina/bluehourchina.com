Before China · Xinjiang transport planning

How to hire a private driver for Altay, Kanas and Yining

For two travellers, the useful question is not one daily price. It is what the quote covers, where the car can go, and whether you need a driver or a separate qualified guide.

Start with the answerSee the quote checklist

Quick answer

Hire the scope, not the headline price

Define exact dates, route order, pickup points, stops, luggage, driver days and language needs before comparing quotes. Ask every provider to answer the same written brief.

A private driver, a transfer with stops and a driver plus separate guide are different scopes. Comparing a single total without those details hides the real differences.

Planning boundary

This checklist is for route planning and quote comparison. It is not a transport booking or confirmation.

Route structure

Compare Altay-Kanas and Yining as two quote blocks

As a planning approach, request one block for Altay, Kanas and Hemu, and another for Yining and Ili. Then compare that with a single-driver proposal for the whole route.

Ask each reply to state the actual pickup and drop-off points, overnight bases, repositioning, hiking trailhead returns and where an official scenic shuttle or vehicle transfer may be required.

Service scope

Driver, guide or both?

Driver only

Vehicle movement, agreed stops, waiting, luggage and transport-day communication.

Transfer with stops

A point-to-point move with written stop duration, waiting and overtime terms.

Separate guide or interpreter

Interpretation, explanation or on-route coordination should be identified and quoted separately.

Hybrid plan

A driver for transport days and a separate language professional only where the route needs one.

Do not treat a driver who knows a few English or Russian phrases as the same service as a qualified guide. Verify the actual person's role and communication ability in writing.

Contracting party

Know who is actually responsible

  • Ask for the contracting company or person and the actual operating provider.
  • Confirm vehicle type, seats, seat belts, luggage capacity and the substitution process.
  • Request deposit, balance, cancellation, refund and receipt terms in writing.
  • Ask who handles a vehicle problem, route change or closure.
  • If a hotel makes the introduction, ask whether it is only a referrer or the responsible contracting party.

Quote checklist

Put every cost and handoff on one page

Send with the request

  • Exact dates and party size
  • Route order and overnight bases
  • Pickup, drop-off and luggage
  • Hiking stops and return times
  • Driver days and language needs

Ask the quote to separate

  • Included hours and distance
  • Fuel, tolls and parking
  • Waiting and overtime
  • Driver meals and accommodation
  • Repositioning or return travel
  • Scenic transfers and exclusions

Compare replies

Protect the route before paying

Red flags include an unnamed contracting party, unclear driver-versus-guide scope, all extras left for later, no cancellation terms, no explanation of scenic-area handoffs, or a vehicle that does not fit the luggage.

Reconfirm before payment

Prices, vehicle availability and language support are confirmed only in a dated written quote. Bluehour does not guarantee a driver, vehicle, guide, road condition or scenic-area access through this page.

Road, weather, permit and scenic-shuttle requirements can change. Confirm the exact route with the relevant transport, scenic-area and exit-entry authorities before paying non-refundable costs.

Do not assume a private vehicle can enter every scenic area. Ask where a transfer to an official shuttle or another vehicle may be required.

FAQ

Questions travellers ask before hiring

How much does a private driver in Xinjiang cost?

There is no safe fixed day rate for every route. Compare dated, itemised quotes with the same scope.

Can one driver cover Altay, Kanas and Yining?

It may be possible. Request both a single-driver option and a two-block comparison before deciding.

Do we need a driver or a guide?

Use a driver for transport and waiting. Define a separate qualified guide or interpreter if you need language and on-route support.

Can our driver enter Kanas Scenic Area?

Do not assume access. Confirm current transfer points, official shuttles, luggage handling and pickup arrangements for your date.

Is an English- or Russian-speaking driver realistic?

You can request it, but directly verify the actual person's ability. Language support and guiding are separate scopes.

Is a hotel-arranged driver safer?

Not automatically. Confirm whether the hotel is only introducing someone or is responsible for the service.

What should two travellers send before requesting a quote?

Send dates, route order, pickup points, nights, luggage, hikes, language needs, driver days and guide needs.
